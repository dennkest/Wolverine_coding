Early Life

Wolverine was born James Howlett between the years 1882 and 1885 in Canada. He discovered his mutant abilities at a young age.

Military Service

Wolverine served in various military conflicts, including World War I and II, where his healing factor and combat skills made him a formidable soldier

Key Life Events

  • Taking up of the name "Logan" to hide his identity
  • Adoption of the codename "Wolverine" from miners in a British Columbia stone quarry
  • Joining the X-Men
  • Participation in major battles and story arcs
